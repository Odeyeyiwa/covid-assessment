Covid-19 Symptoms and Preventives

Wear a mask

Wash hands

Sanitize

Keep a safe distance

Good hygiene

Stay safe by taking some simple precautions, such as physical distancing, wearing a mask, keeping rooms well ventilated, avoiding crowds, cleaning your hands, and coughing into a bent elbow or tissue.

Symptoms of COVID-19 have included:

Cough
Fever
Difficulty breathing
Pain or pressure in the chest
Sore throat
Loss of smell or taste
Diarrhea
Muscle and body aches

*Symptoms range from mild – like the flu and other common respiratory infections – to severe.*
